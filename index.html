mrlinksaverman

An efficient, snappy and straight to the point link management app. Click, sync and get to work.

@mel -from students for students

1. WHY WOULD I EVER NEED THIS?

Most people would say that what this app does is nothing new and the purpose of this can easily be done by copying links n stuffs into a cloud based note apps like google docs or they also say that they can just save the links from group chats to their saved chats on whatsapp or telegram. However, those traditional methods are actually far more sluggish and time consuming than you think. Imagine you're using google docs on your phone to save your links and then boot up your laptop only to wait extra 10 seconds to wait for the website to load extra features that u don't even need for your supposed link bookmark. It's annoying. Similar to using the "Saved Messages" feature in your whatsapp or telegram, over time the links will be lost above other messages that you will be saving and it will just take a lot of time just to find it again. This web app is a more snappy, efficient and organised way to manage your links and just jump right into where you left from your assignments and agendas.

2. WHY DOES IT LACK SO MANY FEATURES?

Some might say that this web app seems so empty and boring. They would say, " you should add something like music player, place where u can add your slides, uploading a profile picture and make it more customisable. However, if I did add all that, it would beat the whole purpose of being fast, compact and straight to the point. Also, if i add some things like music player, i don't think that they're going to use it here since most of them already got a better app for that.

3. HOW ABOUT OUR DATA SECURITY?

Completely secure and private. Your data is protected by enterprise-grade database security, and nobody can see your links but you. How it works: mrlinksaverman connects directly to Supabase (a highly secure, modern cloud database provider). We use Postgres Row-Level Security (RLS). This is a fancy way of saying the database itself acts as a digital vault: it strictly isolates your account so that only your logged-in user ID can read or write your links. Even though the app is built by a student, your data is handled by industry-standard encryption and security protocols. We never sell your data, there are no tracking ads, and your links stay entirely yours.
